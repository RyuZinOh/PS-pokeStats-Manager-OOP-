1. **USER REGISTRATION**
   🚀 Don't have an account yet? Follow these simple steps:
      - Select option 2 in the main menu to register.
      - Enter your desired username.
      - Set a secure password.
      - You're all set!

2. **LOGIN - USER**
   🔐 If you already have an account, choose option 1 from the main menu.
      - Enter your username.
      - Input your password.
      - Enjoy the magic of the Pokemon world in the home menu.

3. **MAIN MENU**
   🎮 Explore exciting options at the main menu:
      - Add Pokemon to the database file for Pokemon market and damage calculator.
      - View your inventory and remove items as needed.
